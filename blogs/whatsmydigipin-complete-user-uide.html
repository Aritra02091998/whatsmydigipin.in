What is DigiPIN?

DigiPIN (Digital Postal Index Number) is India Post's revolutionary addressing system that converts geographic coordinates into a unique 10-character code. Unlike traditional PIN codes that cover large areas, DigiPIN pinpoints locations with 3-5 meter accuracy, transforming how we navigate and deliver across India.

Finding Your DigiPIN with GPS

The easiest way to find your DigiPIN is using your device's GPS location. Here's how:

1

Visit the Homepage

Go to whatsmydigipin.com and scroll to the "Get Your DigiPIN" section.

2

Click "Use My Location"

The system will request permission to access your location. Grant access to proceed.

3

Get Your DigiPIN

Within seconds, your unique 10-character DigiPIN will appear in the display box.

Tip: If you're having trouble with GPS, ensure location services are enabled on your device and browser.

Calculating DigiPIN from Coordinates

If you already know your latitude and longitude, you can manually calculate your DigiPIN:

1

Find Your Coordinates

Use Google Maps or any GPS app to find your exact latitude and longitude.

2

Enter in the Form

Scroll to the "Enter Coordinates Manually" section and input your values.

3

Calculate DigiPIN

Click "Calculate DigiPIN" to instantly generate your digital address code.

Coordinate Ranges for India

  • Latitude Range: 2.5°N to 38.5°N
  • Longitude Range: 63.5°E to 99.5°E
  • Accuracy: 3-5 meters

DigiPIN vs Traditional PIN Code

Understanding the difference between DigiPIN and traditional PIN codes:

Feature Traditional PIN Code DigiPIN
Coverage Area Entire village/town (km²) 4m x 4m location
Accuracy 500m-2km 3-5 meters
Format 6-digit number 10-character alphanumeric
Rural Effectiveness Limited without proper addresses Works without formal addresses
Emergency Use General area only Exact location identification
E-commerce Deliveries Final mile challenges Precise to doorstep

Benefits in Rural India

DigiPIN is transforming rural India by providing precise addressing where traditional systems fail:

  • No Address Needed: Generates location codes without formal street addresses
  • Emergency Services: Ambulances and police can find exact locations during crises
  • Government Services: Accurate delivery of subsidies, pensions and benefits
  • E-commerce Access: Enables reliable deliveries to remote villages
  • Financial Inclusion: Helps banks verify locations for account opening

Case Study: Rural Maharashtra

In villages without formal addresses, DigiPIN reduced delivery times by 65% and increased successful first-attempt deliveries to 98%. Emergency response times improved by 40% during monsoon floods when traditional landmarks were inaccessible.

Using DigiPIN for Emergency Services

DigiPIN can be life-saving during emergencies:

  • Share with Dispatchers: Provide your DigiPIN when calling emergency services
  • No Landmarks Needed: Works where traditional descriptions fail (forests, fields, etc.)
  • Disaster Response: Critical during floods, earthquakes when locations change
  • Medical Emergencies: Ambulances reach exact locations faster
