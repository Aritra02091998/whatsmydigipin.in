What Is a DigiPIN?

A DigiPIN (Digital Postal Index Number) is India Post’s new way of giving every spot in the country a unique 10-character code. Unlike the old 6-digit PIN that lumps whole neighborhoods together, a DigiPIN zeroes in on a precise 3–5 meter square. Think of it as your address in super-high-def.

DigiPIN vs Traditional Address

Still debating digipin vs traditional address? Here’s why DigiPIN wins:

  • Precision: Traditional PIN covers kilometers; DigiPIN covers just 4 m².
  • Speed: Instant lookup vs. manual address entry delays.
  • Reliability: Works in areas without street names or landmarks.
