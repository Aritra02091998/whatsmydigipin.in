What Is a DigiPIN Code?

A DigiPIN is India Post’s new, 10-character digital address for every square meter in the country. Unlike the old 6-digit PIN, which groups entire areas, a DigiPIN zeroes in on tiny spots—ideal for deliveries, emergency services, and location-based apps.

DigiPIN vs Traditional PIN Code Comparison

  • Coverage: Traditional PIN covers kilometers; DigiPIN covers just a few square meters.
  • Precision: GPS-level accuracy vs. block-level grouping.
  • Ease of Use: One click to get a code vs. manual address typing.
